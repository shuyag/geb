Welcome to Robots in Wonderland

We're reimagining a cult classic.

Join us to experience the world of Artificial Intelligence (AI). In this space you can explore topics surrounding ethics and the future of AI, and learn how to contribute to this field whether you are in the early stages or have extensive experience with AI. This site was created for a Harvard Knowledge Design Seminar (Complit 279) as a way to reimagine and communicate the main topics in Douglas Hofstadter's non-fiction book Gödel, Escher and Bach: an Eternal Golden Braid (GEB). This book explores themes in cognitive science and examines the possibility of computers attaining human intelligence. Scroll down to view our site map including topics from GEB and hover over a topic of interest for more information.
